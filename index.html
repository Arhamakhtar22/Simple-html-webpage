How fast can the 2022 BMW M3 accelerate?

The M3 Sedan has a 0-60 mph time of just 4.1 seconds, the M3 Competition boasts a mere 3.8 seconds, while the M3 Competition xDrive has a 3.4 seconds 0-60 mph time.

Is the 2022 BMW M3 an all-wheel drive vehicle?

The 2022 BMW M3 Competition xDrive Sedan features M xDrive, BMW’s performance-oriented all-wheel drive technology, which features adjustable 4WD, 4WD Sport, and 2WD modes for complete control on the road.
